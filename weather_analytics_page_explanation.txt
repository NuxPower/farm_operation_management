ANIBUKID FARM OPERATION MANAGEMENT SYSTEM
=========================================
Detailed Documentation: Weather Reports Page (/weather/analytics)

This document explains every metric, chart, and calculation present on the Weather Reports page. This page focuses on analyzing historical weather patterns and their impact on agronomic conditions.

---------------------------------------------------------
1. GLOBAL FILTERS & CONTROLS
---------------------------------------------------------
- Date Range Dropdown: Filters historical data by "Last 7 Days", "Last 30 Days", "Last 3 Months", or "Last Year".
- Update Report Button: Reloads the dashboard with data for the selected period.
- Export Report Button: Generates and downloads a CSV file containing the raw weather data (Date, Temp, Rainfall, Wind, Sunshine).
- Generate Report Button: Used to generate a formalized report view.

---------------------------------------------------------
2. WEATHER SUMMARY METRICS
---------------------------------------------------------
Averages and totals for the selected time period:
- Avg Temperature: The mean temperature (°C) recorded over the period.
- Total Rainfall: The cumulative amount of precipitation (mm).
- Avg Wind Speed: The average wind velocity (km/h).
- Sunshine Hours: Total estimated hours of direct sunlight.

---------------------------------------------------------
3. HISTORICAL CHARTS
---------------------------------------------------------
- Temperature Trends (Line Chart): Plots daily temperature fluctuations over the selected date range.
- Rainfall Distribution (Bar Chart): Shows the daily accumulation of rainfall, making it easy to identify wet vs. dry spells.

---------------------------------------------------------
4. GROWING DEGREE DAYS (GDD)
---------------------------------------------------------
Measures heat accumulation to estimate crop maturity. (Formula: Average Daily Temp - 10°C Base Temp).
- Today: GDD accumulated on the current day.
- This Week: Cumulative GDD over the past 7 days.
- This Month: Cumulative GDD over the past month.
- This Season: Total GDD accumulated for the current growing season.

---------------------------------------------------------
5. SIGNIFICANT WEATHER EVENTS
---------------------------------------------------------
A chronological log of notable weather occurrences (e.g., Heavy Rain, Storms, Frost, Extreme Heat) that occurred during the period, detailing their Duration, Intensity, and potential Impact.

---------------------------------------------------------
6. HISTORICAL WEATHER DATA (TABLE)
---------------------------------------------------------
A paginated tabular view of daily historical records including: Date, General Condition, Temperature (°C), Rainfall (mm), Humidity (%), and Wind Speed (km/h).

---------------------------------------------------------
7. 7-DAY FORECAST
---------------------------------------------------------
A forward-looking projection for the upcoming week showing:
- Date and Weather Icon.
- General Condition (e.g., Clear, Rain).
- High / Low Temperatures.
- Chance of Rain (%) and Wind Speed.

---------------------------------------------------------
8. WEATHER IMPACT ANALYSIS
---------------------------------------------------------
Translates raw weather data into actionable farming insights:
- Crop Development: Tracks the current Growth Stage, estimated Days to Maturity, and overall Stress Level (Low/Moderate/High) based on weather conditions.
- Field Conditions: Estimates Soil Moisture, Field Workability (suitability for heavy machinery/labor), Disease Risk, and the Data Reliability Score (accuracy confidence of the data).
- Recommendations: Actionable farming advice (e.g., "Postpone spraying due to high winds") derived from the analysis.
