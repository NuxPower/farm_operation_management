ANIBUKID FARM OPERATION MANAGEMENT SYSTEM
=========================================
Analytics Warning Samples and Calculation Guide

This file provides sample warning instances and how each metric is calculated
for:
1) Data Analytics Dashboard (/analytics/data-analysis)
2) Weather Dashboard + Weather Reports (/weather and /weather/analytics)

----------------------------------------------------------------------
A. DATA ANALYTICS WARNING SAMPLES (/analytics/data-analysis)
----------------------------------------------------------------------

A1) Pending Sales Orders Warning
Trigger Rule:
- Warning appears when pending sales orders count > 0.

Calculation:
- pending_orders = count of RiceOrder where status = "pending" for current farmer.

Sample Instance:
- Pending orders found: 4
- Warning text sample:
  "You have 4 pending sales order(s). Review and confirm them."


A2) Pickup Deadline Warning (Urgent)
Trigger Rule:
- Warning appears when order status = "ready_for_pickup" and pickup_deadline is
  within the next 24 hours.

Calculation:
- expiring_pickups = count of ready_for_pickup orders with:
  now < pickup_deadline <= now + 24 hours

Sample Instance:
- 2 pickups expiring in 24 hours
- Warning text sample:
  "2 order(s) awaiting pickup will expire within 24 hours."


A3) Overdue Tasks Warning
Trigger Rule:
- Warning appears when overdue_tasks > 0.

Calculation:
- overdue_tasks = count of farm tasks where:
  due_date < current time AND status != completed

Sample Instance:
- Total tasks: 18
- Completed tasks: 11
- Overdue tasks: 3
- Warning text sample:
  "3 overdue tasks need immediate attention."


A4) Active Pest Incident Warning
Trigger Rule:
- Warning appears when active_incidents > 0.

Calculation:
- active_incidents = count of pest incidents in "active" scope for farm plantings.

Sample Instance:
- Total incidents: 9
- Active incidents: 2
- Warning text sample:
  "2 active pest incidents require treatment."


A5) Inventory Low Stock Warning
Trigger Rule:
- Warning appears when low_stock_count > 0.

Calculation:
- low_stock_count = number of inventory items where item.isLowStock() returns true.

Sample Instance:
- Total inventory items: 31
- Low stock items: 6
- Warning text sample:
  "6 item(s) below minimum stock level - restock needed."


A6) Inventory Expiry Warning
Trigger Rule:
- Warning appears when expiring_soon_count > 0.

Calculation:
- expiring_soon_count = count of inventory items with expiry_date between:
  today and today + 30 days

Sample Instance:
- Expiring soon items: 5
- Warning text sample:
  "5 item(s) expiring within 30 days - use or dispose."


A7) Expense Exceeds Revenue Warning
Trigger Rule:
- Warning appears when total_expenses > total_revenue and revenue > 0.

Calculation:
- profit = total_revenue - total_expenses
- if profit < 0, financial warning is generated.

Sample Instance:
- Revenue: PHP 120,000
- Expenses: PHP 150,000
- Profit: PHP -30,000
- Warning text sample:
  "Expenses exceed revenue. Review expense categories for optimization."


A8) Field Utilization Monitoring (Operational Risk Signal)
Trigger Rule:
- No strict hard alert in code, but low utilization indicates underused land.

Calculation:
- total_area = sum(field.size)
- planted_area = sum(planting.area_planted for statuses planted/growing/active)
- utilization_rate = (planted_area / total_area) * 100

Sample Instance:
- total_area = 20.0 ha
- planted_area = 8.0 ha
- utilization_rate = (8.0 / 20.0) * 100 = 40.0%
- Risk interpretation sample:
  "Land utilization is low (40.0%). Review planting schedule."


A9) Labor Completion Rate (Productivity Risk Signal)
Trigger Rule:
- No direct hard warning threshold, but low completion rate indicates labor risk.

Calculation:
- completion_rate = (completed_tasks / total_tasks) * 100

Sample Instance:
- completed_tasks = 11
- total_tasks = 18
- completion_rate = (11 / 18) * 100 = 61.1%
- Risk interpretation sample:
  "Task completion rate is 61.1%. Consider labor/task reallocation."


A10) Post-Harvest Recovery Performance
Trigger Rule:
- No direct warning threshold, but low recovery rate or high provider cost can be flagged.

Calculation:
- process_recovery_rate = (output_quantity / input_quantity) * 100 (from process method)
- average_recovery_rate = mean(process_recovery_rate across completed processes)
- self_avg_cost = average(cost/output_quantity for self-processed records)
- provider_avg_cost = average(cost/output_quantity for provider-processed records)

Sample Instance:
- Process A recovery: 63%
- Process B recovery: 68%
- Process C recovery: 64%
- average_recovery_rate = (63 + 68 + 64) / 3 = 65.0%
- self_avg_cost = PHP 4.20/unit
- provider_avg_cost = PHP 6.10/unit
- Risk interpretation sample:
  "Provider processing is costlier than self-processing by PHP 1.90/unit."

  "High Wind Advisory: High wind speeds (26 km/h) detected."

C. WEATHER ANALYTICS CALCULATIONS (FORMULAS + EXAMPLES)
----------------------------------------------------------------------

C1) Weather Summary Calculations (Reports Weather)
- avg_temperature = average(weather_log.temperature over selected period)
- total_rainfall = sum(weather_log.rainfall over selected period)
- avg_wind_speed = average(weather_log.wind_speed over selected period)
- sunshine_hours = clear_days * 8 (estimated)

Example:
- Temperatures: [28, 30, 29, 31] => avg = 29.5 C
- Rainfall: [5, 0, 12, 3] => total = 20 mm
- Wind: [9, 11, 13, 7] => avg = 10.0 km/h
- Clear days: 2 => sunshine_hours = 2 * 8 = 16 h


C2) GDD (Growing Degree Days) - Reports Backend
Formula:
- daily_gdd = max(0, daily_avg_temp - 10)
- today/week/month/season GDD are sums of daily_gdd over each window.

Example:
- Daily average temperatures for week: [26, 27, 28, 25, 24, 29, 30]
- Daily GDD: [16, 17, 18, 15, 14, 19, 20]
- Week GDD = 16+17+18+15+14+19+20 = 119


C3) Dashboard GDD (Frontend Approximation)
Formula used in dashboard widget:
- daily_gdd = max(0, ((high + low) / 2) - 10)
- week = sum(next 7 days daily_gdd
- month ~= (weekly average scaled) * 4.3
- season ~= month * 4

Example:
- Day high/low = 34/24
- daily_gdd = ((34 + 24) / 2) - 10 = 19


C4) Rain Chance Conversion (ColorfulClouds path)
If precipitation amount is used:
- 0 < rain < 1 mm => rain_chance = 20%
- 1 to <5 mm => 50%
- 5 to <10 mm => 70%
- >=10 mm => 90%

Example:
- Predicted rain = 6.5 mm => rain_chance = 70%


C5) Disease Risk Indicator (Weather Impact)
In weather impact mapping:
- disease risk uses assessed disease risk levels and maps highest level:
  high -> High, medium -> Moderate, else Low

Example:
- Rice blast overall risk: medium
- Bacterial blight overall risk: high
- Final disease_risk shown = High


----------------------------------------------------------------------
D. QUICK REFERENCE WARNING THRESHOLDS
----------------------------------------------------------------------

Data Analytics:
- Pending orders warning: pending_orders > 0
- Pickup expiry warning: pickup_deadline within next 24h
- Overdue tasks warning: overdue_tasks > 0
- Pest warning: active_incidents > 0
- Low stock warning: low_stock_count > 0
- Expiry warning: expiring_soon_count > 0
- Financial concern: total_expenses > total_revenue
- Planning load note: pending_tasks > 5

Weather Analytics:
- Extreme heat: temperature > 35 C
- Cold stress: temperature < 15 C
- Strong wind: > 15 km/h (rice-specific), > 20 km/h (dashboard local advisory)
- High humidity disease risk: humidity > 85%
- Forecast rain warning: rain/storm condition OR rain chance > 50%
- Forecast heat warning: max temp > 35 C
- Flowering critical heat: stage flowering AND temp > 30 C
- Flowering low humidity: stage flowering AND humidity < 60%
- Grain filling heat stress: stage grain_filling AND temp > 32 C
