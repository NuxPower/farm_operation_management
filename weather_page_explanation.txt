ANIBUKID FARM OPERATION MANAGEMENT SYSTEM
=========================================
Detailed Documentation: Weather Dashboard Page (/weather)

This document explains the features, metrics, and widgets available on the real-time Weather Dashboard. This page is designed for day-to-day monitoring of immediate weather conditions affecting the farm.

---------------------------------------------------------
1. GLOBAL CONTROLS
---------------------------------------------------------
- Refresh Button: Pulls the latest real-time weather data from the weather API.
- View Forecast Button: Navigates the user to the detailed Weather Reports & Analytics page (/weather/analytics).

---------------------------------------------------------
2. CURRENT WEATHER OVERVIEW
---------------------------------------------------------
Displays the immediate, real-time meteorological conditions for the farm location:
- Temperature: Current air temperature (°C).
- Humidity: Current relative humidity percentage (%).
- Rainfall (24h): Cumulative precipitation over the last 24 hours (mm).
- Wind Speed: Current wind velocity (km/h).

---------------------------------------------------------
3. INTERACTIVE WEATHER MAP
---------------------------------------------------------
An embedded, interactive map (powered by Windy.com) centered on the farm's coordinates.
- Layer Controls: Allows the user to toggle different meteorological overlays on the map:
    * Wind: Visualizes wind currents and speed.
    * Rain: Displays precipitation radar.
    * Temperature: Shows a heatmap of regional temperatures.
    * Clouds: Displays cloud cover.

---------------------------------------------------------
4. 7-DAY FORECAST
---------------------------------------------------------
A daily summary of expected weather for the upcoming week.
- For each day, it displays: Date, Icon, General Condition (e.g., Partly Cloudy), High/Low Temperatures, Chance of Rain (%), and Wind Speed.
- View Hourly (Modal): Clicking this opens a detailed breakdown showing predicted Temperature, Condition, and Rain Chance for every hour of that specific day.

---------------------------------------------------------
5. WEATHER ALERTS
---------------------------------------------------------
A prioritized feed of warnings and advisories designed to protect farm operations.
- Generates alerts from three sources:
    1. Official weather service alerts (if available in the API).
    2. Local Current Conditions: Triggers warnings for immediate threats like extreme heat (>35°C), high winds (>20 km/h), or active rain/storms.
    3. Upcoming Forecast: Scans the next 3 days to provide advance warning for expected heavy rain or extreme heat.
- Alerts are color-coded by severity (Danger/Red, Warning/Yellow, Info/Blue).

---------------------------------------------------------
6. GROWING DEGREE DAYS (GDD) SUMMARY
---------------------------------------------------------
A quick-reference widget for heat accumulation, vital for tracking rice development.
- Shows GDD accumulated: Today, This Week, This Month, and This Season.
- Includes an informational tooltip explaining the GDD formula: ((Max Temp + Min Temp) / 2) - 10°C, and notes that rice typically requires ~2200 cumulative GDD to mature.

---------------------------------------------------------
7. QUICK ACTIONS
---------------------------------------------------------
- Export Weather Data: Downloads a CSV of recent farm weather records.
- View Weather Reports: Shortcut to the historical analytics page.
